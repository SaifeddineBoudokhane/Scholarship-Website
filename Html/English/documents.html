Scholarship Application Checklist for International Students


Congratulations on considering Tunisia for your higher education journey! Below is a checklist of documents required for international students applying to bachelor's degree programs in Tunisia. Please ensure that you have all necessary documents ready before starting your application process.


1. Application Form:

Complete and submit the online application form available on the Tunisia Universities Scholarships website.



2. Educational Credentials:


For the Bachelor's degree application:


  • Baccalauréat Diploma: Certified copy of your high school diploma or leaving certificate, indicating successful completion of secondary education.


For the Master's degree application:


  • Bachelor's Degree Transcript: Certified copies of transcripts from your undergraduate studies, including courses taken and grades earned


  • Bachelor's Degree Certificate: Certified copy of your bachelor's degree certificate or diploma, indicating successful completion of your undergraduate program.


For the Doctorate application:


  • Master's Degree Transcript: Certified copies of transcripts from your graduate studies, including courses taken and grades earned


  • Master's Degree Certificate: Certified copy of your master's degree certificate or diploma, indicating successful completion of your graduate program.



3. Language Proficiency Test Scores:

  • English Proficiency: If your chosen program is taught in English, provide proof of English proficiency through standardized tests such as TOEFL or IELTS. Minimum scores may vary by institution.


  • French Proficiency: If your chosen program is taught in French, provide proof of French proficiency through standardized tests such as DELF or DALF.



4. Curriculum Vitae (CV) or Resume:

Provide a comprehensive CV or resume outlining your academic background, research experience, publications, conference presentations, teaching experience, and relevant skills.



5. Research Proposal (For master's and Doctorate Application):

Submit a detailed research proposal outlining your proposed doctoral research topic, objectives, methodology, theoretical framework, and expected contributions to the field.



6. Personal Statement or Essay:

Write a compelling personal statement outlining your academic interests, career goals, and reasons for choosing Tunisia for your higher education journey.



7. Letters of Recommendation:

Submit two or three letters of recommendation from teachers, counselors, or other academic sources who can attest to your academic abilities and character.



8. Financial Documents:

Provide proof of financial means to cover tuition fees, living expenses, and other costs during your stay in Tunisia. This may include bank statements, sponsorship letters, or scholarship awards.



9. Medical Certificate:

Submit a medical certificate signed by a licensed physician, attesting to your physical and mental fitness for studying abroad.



10. Passport:

A valid passport with a minimum of six months validity beyond the intended period of stay in Tunisia.



11. Passport-Sized Photographs:

Recent passport-sized photographs according to the university's specifications.



12. Visa Documents:

Once admitted, you will need to apply for a student visa. Prepare the necessary documents, including a visa application form, acceptance letter from the university, proof of financial means, and health insurance coverage.



13. Additional Documents:

Check with your chosen university for any additional documents required for admission, such as essays, portfolios, or proof of extracurricular activities.




For further assistance or inquiries, please don't hesitate to contact the Tunisian Universities Scholarships team. We're here to support you throughout your application process and beyond!
