Master's


The second cycle, known as the Master's, delves deeper into specialized areas of study, fostering advanced knowledge and research skills.


Master's Duration:

Typically spanning two academic years, the Master's program emphasizes in-depth exploration and research within the chosen field.


Research Focus:

Core and Elective Courses:


Students engage in a variety of core and elective courses, allowing them to tailor their education to their specific interests. This multidisciplinary approach ensures a well-rounded skill set.


Thesis or Project:


Master's candidates often engage in extensive research, culminating in a thesis or project that contributes to the academic community.



Assessment and Grading:

Research Evaluation:


Assessment in Master's programs is often centered around the quality and contribution of the candidate's research work.


Grading System:


Tunisian universities commonly use a grading scale ranging from 0 to 20, with 10 considered a passing grade.


Available Master's Degree Fields of Study

Master's Degree Scholarships cover a wide range of disciplines, offering opportunities in various fields such as:

  • Engineering: Advanced Materials Engineering, Computer Networks, Renewable Energy.

  • Sciences: Molecular Biology, Data Science, Environmental Sciences.

  • Humanities: Comparative Literature, Linguistics, Cultural Studies.

  • Business and Economics: International Business Management, Financial Engineering, Entrepreneurship.

  • Social Sciences: Sustainable Development, Political Science, Psychology.

  • Health Sciences: Public Health, Biomedical Engineering, Clinical Research.

  • Fine Arts and Design: Digital Arts, Urban Design, Architecture.
