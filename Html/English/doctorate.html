Doctorate (Ph.D.)


The third cycle, known as the Doctorate or Ph.D., represents the pinnacle of academic achievement. Candidates engage in original and extensive research, making significant contributions to their field.


Doctorate Duration:

Typically lasting for two to three years, Doctorate programs involve in-depth research, publication of findings, and the completion of a doctoral thesis.


Research Focus:

Original Research:


Doctoral candidates conduct original and groundbreaking research, advancing knowledge in their chosen discipline.


Thesis Defense:


Many Bachelor's programs incorporate practical training, internships, or projects, enabling students to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios.



Assessment and Grading:

Continuous Assessment:


Evaluation often involves a combination of exams, assignments, presentations, and project work, promoting continuous learning and engagement.


Grading System:


Tunisian universities commonly use a grading scale ranging from 0 to 20, with 10 considered a passing grade.


Available Doctorate Fields of Study

Our Doctorate scholarships are designed to support international scholars in pursuing high-level research and academic excellence in Tunisia.

  • Engineering: Advanced Materials Engineering, Robotics, Sustainable Energy.

  • Sciences: Biomedical Research, Astrophysics, Environmental Sciences.

  • Humanities: Literary Criticism, Archaeology, Cultural Studies.

  • Business and Economics: Strategic Management, Economic Policy, Financial Economics.

  • Social Sciences: Political Philosophy, Sociology of Globalization, International Relations.

  • Health Sciences: Medical Research, Public Health, Pharmaceutical Sciences.
